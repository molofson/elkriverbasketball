• Huddle on the Hardwood

    2017 3v3 Tournament Registration

  • The Elk River Huddle on the Hardwood 3v3 Tourney is open to participants from any community. This will be a double-elimination style basketball tournament held on Saturday, July 29th.

    Team costs are due at time of registration and will be based on the number of registered players on the team ($25/player), which cover the tournament costs and fund the boys and girls youth basketball scholarship programs.

    Teams are responsible for providing their own game jerseys (same color shirts for all players are acceptable). Teams are also encouraged to get creative with their names and jerseys to stand out and show their style!

    Walk-up registration is still open; however, online registration is closed and event T-shirts are no longer available for registrants.

    If someone wants to play but doesn't have a team, register on-site by 10:00 AM on the 29th and we will try to place you on a team or make a team with other walk up players who want to join.

    Based on current registration numbers, we will be combining many of our divisions. We will do our best to keep the divisions as fair as we can make them.

    Tentative brackets and rules will be emailed to all registered teams by mid week. The schedule of the tourney being is tentatively set to run from 10:30 AM to 6:00 PM.

    More event information can be found here.

    You or your family don't need to be signed up for the 3v3 tourney to enjoy the day. There are many more outside activities. Bring a tent and tail gate around the hoops in the parking lot.
