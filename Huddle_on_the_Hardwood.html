• Huddle on the Hardwood

    2017 3v3 Tournament Registration

  • The Elk River Huddle on the Hardwood 3V3 Tourney is open to participants from any community. This will be a 3 on 3 double-elimination style basketball tournament.

    Team costs are due at time of registration and will be based on the number of registered players on the team ($25/player), which cover the tournament costs and fund the boys and girls youth basketball scholarship program.

    Teams are responsible for providing their own game jerseys (same color shirts for all players are acceptable). Teams are also encouraged to get creative with their names and jerseys to stand out and show their style!

    Deadline to register your team is July 16th. Late registrations will not guaranteed an event T-shirt.

    Divisions are subject to change based on the numbers of registering teams.
